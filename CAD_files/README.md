CAD files used in the project:

- Arm Mounting Plate: supports myCobot 280 pi robot arm and the Intel RealSense D435 RGBD Camera
- NUC Mounting Plate: supports Intel NUC 12
- Camera mount: holds RealSense D435 RGBD Camera. Camera will be oriented at 2.5 degrees downwards.
- Lidar Mount: hold RPLiDAR A2

Both mounting plates are elevated at 50mm to clear the Lidar's Field of View
